Learn More About Us.

At Pangea Society, we are committed to bridging the gap between academia, industry, and policy. Our mission is to drive impactful change by fostering collaboration between top researchers, industry leaders, and policymakers. We provide researchers with the resources they need to address real-world challenges, offer cutting-edge solutions to industry problems, and help policymakers implement informed, sustainable practices. Through partnerships, events, and a global network of experts, we strive to create a platform that elevates academia and research in India, inspiring the next generation of problem-solvers.

Who

Pangea Society is a forward-thinking organization bringing together the brightest minds in academia, industry, and policy-making. Its fellows include experts who are passionate about structured research, solving complex problems, and driving impactful change across various sectors.

When

The Pangea Society was established to address a growing need for collaboration between academic institutions, industries, and policymakers. It aims to bridge these sectors at a global level, focusing on significant societal challenges and creating long-term solutions.

What

The organization's primary goal is to foster interdisciplinary discussions and provide grants to researchers while solving pressing industry problems. Pangea also works to elevate India's academic landscape by building partnerships and offering a platform for conferences, webinars, and collaborations.

How

Pangea facilitates research-backed solutions by connecting industries with academic fellows. These fellows, experts in their fields, collaborate with industry partners, develop practical solutions, and engage in impactful projects. This collaboration not only addresses immediate challenges but also drives long-term development across multiple sectors.
